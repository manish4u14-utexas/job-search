# Daily Job Search Workflow Guide

## ✅ Your Dashboard is Ready!

Your job search dashboard is fully functional and optimized for daily job hunting. Here's how to use it effectively:

---

## 🌅 Morning Routine (9-10 AM)

### 1. **Check Indeed** (10-15 jobs)
- Open dashboard → Switch to **Indeed** tab
- Set time filter: **24 hours** (fresh jobs from yesterday)
- Set jobs limit: **10-15 jobs**
- Click **REFRESH**
- Review jobs with AI match scores
- **Visa Sponsorship** is highlighted (red/green/orange)

### 2. **Check LinkedIn** (10-15 jobs)
- Switch to **LinkedIn** tab
- Set time filter: **24 hours**
- Set jobs limit: **10-15 jobs**
- Click **REFRESH**
- Review jobs with AI match scores

### 3. **Apply to Top Matches** (80%+ match)
For each high-match job:
1. Click **ANALYZE MATCH** to see detailed breakdown
2. Click **TAILOR RESUME** to get customized resume
3. Click **COPY TO CLIPBOARD** to copy tailored resume
4. Click **APPLY NOW** to open job posting
5. Submit application with tailored resume

**Expected time:** 30-45 minutes

## 🎯 Application Strategy

### Priority Levels:

**🟢 MUST APPLY (80-100% match)**
- Strong skills alignment
- Experience matches perfectly
- Location fits
- **Visa sponsorship offered or not mentioned**
- Apply within 24 hours

**🟡 SHOULD APPLY (60-79% match)**
- Good skills overlap
- Some experience gaps (addressable)
- Location acceptable
- Check visa sponsorship status
- Apply within 48 hours

**🔴 CONSIDER (40-59% match)**
- Partial skills match
- Stretch role (growth opportunity)
- Review carefully before applying
- Apply if genuinely interested

## 💡 Pro Tips

### 1. **Token Management** (Groq Free Tier)
- **Daily limit:** 100,000 tokens
- **Morning:** 10-15 jobs = ~15,000-30,000 tokens
- **Afternoon:** 5 jobs = ~5,000-10,000 tokens
- **Evening:** 5 jobs = ~5,000-10,000 tokens
- **Total:** ~25,000-50,000 tokens/day
- **Buffer:** 50,000 tokens for resume tailoring

**If you hit rate limit:**
- Wait 30 minutes, or
- Switch to OpenAI in Settings (paid but reliable)

### 2. **Resume Tailoring**
- Use **TAILOR RESUME** for top matches only (saves tokens)
- Keep tailored resumes in a folder organized by company
- Reuse similar tailored resumes for similar roles

### 3. **Visa Sponsorship Focus**
- ✅ **Green:** "Visa sponsorship offered" → APPLY IMMEDIATELY
- ⚠️ **Orange:** "Not mentioned" → APPLY (ask during interview)
- ❌ **Red:** "No sponsorship" → Skip unless exceptional match

### 4. **Track Your Applications**
- Keep a spreadsheet with:
  - Company name
  - Job title
  - Date applied
  - Match score
  - Visa sponsorship status
  - Follow-up date
  - Status (applied/interview/rejected/offer)

### 5. **Optimize Your Profile**
- Update skills based on trending requirements
- Add new certifications or projects
- Refresh resume every 2 weeks
- Keep job title preferences current

## 🚨 Troubleshooting

### "No jobs found"
- ✅ Check if rate limit was hit (pink banner)
- ✅ Try different time filter (48h or 72h)
- ✅ Reduce jobs limit (try 5 instead of 10)
- ✅ Check your profile has job titles and locations

### "Rate limit reached"
- ✅ Wait 30 minutes for Groq to reset
- ✅ Switch to OpenAI in Settings
- ✅ Reduce jobs limit for next search
- ✅ Use fewer searches per day

### "Jobs not matching"
- ✅ Review your profile skills
- ✅ Update job title preferences
- ✅ Expand target locations
- ✅ Lower match threshold (40% is current)

### "No visa sponsorship info"
- ✅ Most jobs don't mention it explicitly
- ✅ Orange status = worth applying
- ✅ Ask during phone screen
- ✅ Check company's H1B history (myvisajobs.com)
