## 🎓 How to Use

### **First Time Setup:**
1. Go to **Profile** page
2. Upload your resume
3. Review auto-parsed information
4. Add/edit job titles you're looking for
5. Add target locations
6. Save profile

### **Daily Job Search:**
1. Open dashboard
2. Select **Indeed** or **LinkedIn** tab
3. Choose time filter (24h recommended for daily)
4. Choose jobs limit (10-15 for morning, 5 for quick checks)
5. Click **REFRESH**
6. Review jobs with match scores
7. For high matches (80%+):
   - Click **ANALYZE MATCH** for details
   - Click **TAILOR RESUME** for customized resume
   - Click **COPY TO CLIPBOARD**
   - Click **APPLY NOW** to open job posting
   - Submit application

### **If Rate Limit Hit:**
1. Pink warning banner will show
2. Toast notification will appear
3. Options:
   - Wait 30 minutes for Groq to reset
   - Switch to OpenAI in Settings (paid)
   - Reduce jobs limit for next search
   - Come back later

   - **Solution:** Use as guidance, apply your judgment

   - **Solution:** Always verify during interview process
