# 💰 Railway Pricing Explained Simply

## 🎯 What "$5 Credit" Means

**Railway gives you $5 worth of resources every month for FREE.**

Think of it like a prepaid phone card:
- You get $5 loaded every month
- You "spend" it based on usage
- When you use resources, it deducts from your $5
- Next month, it resets to $5 again

---

## 📊 What You Pay For (Usage-Based)

Railway charges based on **resources used**, NOT number of visits:

### **1. Compute (CPU + RAM)**
**What it is:** The server running your app

**Cost:** ~$0.000463 per GB-hour
- Your app uses ~512MB RAM
- Running 24/7 = 720 hours/month
- **Cost: ~$1.50-2.00/month**

**NOT affected by:**
- ❌ Number of visitors
- ❌ Number of page views
- ❌ How many times you access it

**Affected by:**
- ✅ How much RAM your app uses
- ✅ How many hours it runs

### **2. Storage (Database)**
**What it is:** Disk space for SQLite database

**Cost:** ~$0.25 per GB/month
- Your database: ~100MB (0.1GB)
- **Cost: ~$0.03/month**

**NOT affected by:**
- ❌ Number of database queries
- ❌ Number of visitors

**Affected by:**
- ✅ Size of your database

### **3. Network (Bandwidth)**
**What it is:** Data transferred in/out

**Cost:** ~$0.10 per GB
- First 100GB FREE
- Your app: ~1-2GB/month
- **Cost: $0/month** (within free limit)

**Affected by:**
- ✅ Number of visitors
- ✅ Size of pages/images
- ✅ API calls

## 🎯 What Affects Your Cost

### **Increases Cost:**
1. ✅ **Running 24/7** (but necessary)
   - Cost: ~$1.50/month
   - Can't reduce (need always-on)

2. ✅ **More RAM usage**
   - If app uses 1GB instead of 512MB
   - Cost doubles to ~$3/month
   - Your app is optimized, won't happen

3. ✅ **Large database**
   - If database grows to 10GB
   - Cost: ~$2.50/month
   - Unlikely for personal use

4. ✅ **Heavy traffic**
   - If 100GB+ bandwidth/month
   - Cost: ~$10/month
   - Won't happen for personal use

### **Does NOT Increase Cost:**
- ❌ Number of times YOU access it
- ❌ Number of job searches
- ❌ Number of resume tailoring
- ❌ Number of page views (within reason)
- ❌ How long you stay on the site

## 💡 Simple Explanation

**Think of it like electricity:**

### **Your Home Electricity Bill:**
```
You pay for:
- ✅ Appliances running (compute)
- ✅ How long they run (hours)
- ✅ How much power they use (RAM)

You DON'T pay for:
- ❌ How many times you turn on lights
- ❌ How many people use the lights
- ❌ How long lights stay on (already counted)
```

### **Railway Hosting:**
```
You pay for:
- ✅ Server running (compute)
- ✅ How long it runs (24/7)
- ✅ Resources it uses (RAM/storage)

You DON'T pay for:
- ❌ How many times you visit
- ❌ How many people visit
- ❌ How many searches you do
```
